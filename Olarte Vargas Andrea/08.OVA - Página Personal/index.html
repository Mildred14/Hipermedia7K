About me

I am a 21-year-old Mexican woman about to become a software engineer and who enjoys to code and work on mobile and web projects. Right now I am looking for settling down in Canada, the country I fell in love with because of the weather, landscapes, people and everything.

Since I was a kid I love math so I enjoyed every math class at school I had and I felt lucky for not having to deal with the pain of studying for it. I loved the feeling and process of solving a problem and the hunger of that put me many times into participating on different types of national, regional and local math contest, getting to win some good places.

Another interesting fact about me is that I have been working my whole life -since I was 6 years old- as a waitress in a family taco place and even if sometimes is tiring I really enjoy it.
